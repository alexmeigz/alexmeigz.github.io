Foodtography & Photography
If you couldn’t tell already, I am a HUGE foodie. I love cooking and presenting food; I love to try new food; I also love to capture the essence of foods in a snapshot to share with the world. Below, you can see a small gallery of my Homemade food, as well as the best of my samples across numerous restaurants. Through cooking, I developed a passion for photography, and I’ve expanded to capturing special moments in nature (in particular), a sampling of which can be viewed below.
