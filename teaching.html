Teaching

A big part of my life is helping others foster their passion for technology, especially those who may not have the resources financially to get adequate exposure. For this reason, I have headed free several programs to bolster technological exposure for underrepresented minorities. In addition, I also teach via other programs to promote students' confidence in the field of computer science and help them focus on its extraordinary applications.
