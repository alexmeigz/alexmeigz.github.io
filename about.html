About Me

Hello there! My name is Alex Mei and I am a current second-year student at the University of California: Santa Barbara majoring in Computer Science and minoring in Statistical Sciences. Before then, I resided in China during my early years before moving to Californiaâ€™s Bay Area for the latter majority of my life.

At the core of Silicon Valley, I fostered a love for Computer Science, volunteering to teach teenagers at two local classes introductory Python and Computer Science principles using the NAO robot respectively. Through my heartwarming experiences, I decided to pursue Computer Science further professionally, leading me to UCSB today.

In Santa Barbara, I am participating in the Early Research Scholars Program for a first-hand experience in research as well as applying my CS mentoring background by participating as an Undergraduate Learning Assistant and volunteering with the local libraries and schools to foster students' technical passion.

Outside the realm of academics, I nurtured a passion for food with my Cantonese heritage. From the intersection of coding and food my cooking website, alexmeicooking.com, came about, where I aim to share innovative, healthy, delicious recipes to get people to step outside their comfort zone and into the kitchen. This derived my passion for food presentation, food photography, and photography in general.
